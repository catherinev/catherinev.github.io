Group Projects

To be perfectly honest, the real reason I want to write this post is so I can show off the amazing website my group made last weekend. I think everyone can agree it has the best web design seen since the late 90s.

As long as I'm writing this post, I might as well take the opportunity to mention group work at DBC. Each week we are assigned a new group of 3-4 people. On Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday, each group splits into pairs and the pairs work on core challenges. Thursday is assessment day/optional pairing day (after assessment, we can choose to pair or work solo). On Friday, the whole group comes together to work on one big project. The projects have included writing a Sudoku solver, a flashcard app, a To-Do list application, and our own version of Survey Monkey (see excellent results above).    read more »

Some reflections on Phase 2

First, apologies for not posting more over the past few weeks. Phase 2 was pretty intense. But I have a bit of a break this evening and am hoping to post a few of the things I've worked on!

Despite the long hours, I really enjoyed phase 2. The first week was pretty stressful, and I constantly felt behind. But partway through the second week I realized that I was learning more each day than I ever have before. I didn't even know I was capable of fitting so much new information into my brain at one time.
