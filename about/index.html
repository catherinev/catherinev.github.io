About me

I am a software engineer at Narrative Science. I have a masters degree in math from the University of Illinois at Chicago and graduated from Dev Bootcamp in the summer of 2014. For more than 10 years, I taught math to students of all ages and levels. I now enjoy applying my communications, management, and mentorship skills to software development projects.

In my spare time, I like to eat and experiment with food. I especially like cheese and chocolate (but not together). Sometimes I run, but not very far. I also like to make things like quilts.
