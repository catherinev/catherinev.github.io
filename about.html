About me

I have a masters degree in math and am learning to be a web developer at Dev Bootcamp. In my spare time, I like to eat and experiment with food. I especially like cheese and chocolate (but not together). Sometimes I run, but not very far. I also like to make things, like quilts.

For more than 10 years, I have taught math to students of all ages and levels. I look forward to applying some of my communications, management, and mentorship skills to web development projects.

This website is about me and my ongoing journey as I learn to program and develop awesome web products.
