All about Maru the Cat

Maru is a famous internet cat. Have you heard of him?
If not, you must be living under a rock (or under a box...?).

Luckily, you can learn the most important things you need to know about Maru here!

Some biographical information

  • Maru lives in Japan
  • Maru is a Scottish Fold cat
  • Maru's owner goes by mugumogu
  • "Maru" means "round" in Japanese--he got the name because of his fluffy round face
  • Maru is 6 years old
  • Maru loves boxes

Maru and Boxes

The most important thing to know about Maru is that he loves boxes. Maru can fit into boxes that are much smaller than he is. If you don't believe me, you can see for youself. It's a very impressive skill.
