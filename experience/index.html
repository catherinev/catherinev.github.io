Don't Park There Chicago

A Rails app that informs users of street sweeping dates in their areas. I was part of a team of 5 people who built the application in less than 8 days. The app uses geospatial data about Chicago's 888 street sweeping regions to render an interactive Leaflet map. Users may sign up for email notifications (powered by Mandrill) or text notifications (powered by Twilio) to avoid being ticketed. The site is responsive and optimized for use on computers, smartphones, and other mobile devices.
