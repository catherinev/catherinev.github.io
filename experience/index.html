Highlighted Projects

Don't Park There Chicago

dontparkthere.herokuapp.com

A Rails app that informs users of street sweeping dates in their areas. The app uses geospatial data about Chicago's 888 street sweeping regions to render an interactive Leaflet map. Users may sign up for email notifications (powered by Mandrill) or text notifications (powered by Twilio) to avoid being ticketed.

DBC Overflow

honeybadgeroverflow.herokuapp.com/

DBC Overflow is an app for Dev Bootcamp staff, students, and alumni to provide online support to each other in the form of posted questions and answers, as well as comments on each of those questions and/or answers. While the information is available to the public, the ability to post questions, answers, or comments is limited to registered users.

DBC Overflow is similar to stackoverflow in that it provides a persistent repository for technical questions and answers, but slightly different in that its categories for questions will also include areas such as Engineering Empathy, Logistics, etc. Questions and support regarding specific topics and challenges are also encouraged.
